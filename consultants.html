    • Jason Reed Wagner

      Founding Partner

      Jason is a founding partner at Reed Hamilton. He specializes in financial services search assignments covering global banking & markets, asset & wealth management and real estate across Asia’s main banking hubs – Hong Kong, Singapore and Tokyo. He has also worked on numerous assignments in Asia’s emerging markets such as Thailand, Indonesia & Korea. He brings more than 20 years of international experience, with 16 of those years spent living and working in Asia.

Throughout his career, he has focused on business building in emerging/fast growth markets specializing in capability deployment, acquisition integration, and operational improvement.
