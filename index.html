Our Firm

Reed Hamilton is a boutique management consulting and executive search firm based in Singapore. We are experts in talent management in your industry because we come from your industry.
We understand your business and the challenges in sourcing high-caliber talent in the dynamic fast growth markets of Asia. Every client and candidate is important to us.

We are small and because of this, pragmatic no-nonsense client partnering is the cornerstone of what we do. Our access to executives comes from over 35 years of combined industry experience in the region. We offer unique agility in accessing top talent by leveraging our extensive contact network and state of the art technology. We are results driven with a client centric approach that ensures quality of execution.

Our primary practices are financial services and technology with particular focus on:

  • Executive search
  • Market entry ramp-up and acquisition take-down
  • Interim management for market entry/turn around situations
  • Medium & long term project and program management

Practices

  • Financial Services

    Our financial services practice is primarily focused on senior leadership in the operations, technology, finance and risk management disciplines.

    Our coverage includes retail, commercial and investment banking as well as non-bank financial institutions such as asset management, insurance, real estate & private equity.

    Our consultants come from the industry and have success partnering with major clients at both the regional and global level.

  • Technology

    Our technology practice brings a distinctive solution to the market by focusing on the core strengths of our team. Our areas of concentration include emerging and environmental technologies, cloud computing, new media, mobility applications, social networking, big-data, in memory computing, analytics, and core software.

    Our consulting team come directly from senior management in industry leading organizations and have acumen in building teams in sales, marketing, operations, business development, and general management. From market entry and go-to-market design and staffing, to succession candidates and team leadership, we are uniquely capable of delivering speed, agility, and success to your search for top talent in Asia.

  • Communications

    We leverage our teams collective experience and time in the field to deliver value in the geographies we cover, fast growth Asian markets.

    The objective of our fast growth markets coverage is to help organizations build robust sales, marketing, and operations execution engines; building foundations that address their specific needs and challenges, enabling them to fully capitalize on the growth opportunities in our region.

    Our expertise and experience in developing businesses and go-to-market strategies across the countries of South East, North, and South Asia allow us to bring deep networks and sought after talent to our clients.

    • Jason Reed Wagner

      Founding Partner

      Jason Reed Wagner is a founding partner at Reed Hamilton. He specializes in financial services search assignments covering global banking & markets, asset & wealth management and real estate across Asia’s main banking hubs – Hong Kong, Singapore and Tokyo. He has also worked on numerous assignments in Asia’s emerging markets such as Thailand, Indonesia & Korea. He brings more than 19 years of international experience, with 15 of those years spent living and working in Asia.

      Prior to co-founding Reed Hamilton, Jason was a search consultant with Spencer Stuart based in their Singapore office. Prior to entering the search industry, he was a senior vice president with the Fortress Investment Group based in Europe where he was head of operations for Germany’s largest commercial REIT and head of acquisition integration for one of Europe’s largest distressed debt servicers. Prior to Fortress, He was a consultant with McKinsey & Company’s business building practice based in Singapore. Prior to McKinsey, he was chief information officer for a Goldman Sachs & GE Capital Commercial Finance joint venture in Thailand immediately following the Asian economic crisis. Throughout his career, he has focused on business building in emerging/fast growth markets specializing in capability deployment, acquisition integration, and operational improvement.

      Jason graduated from the University of Florida with a degree in Asian studies and has a Certificate of Professional Development from the Wharton Business School at the University of Pennsylvania. He is a citizen of Singapore and is conversant in Japanese.

    • Charles Hamilton Ferguson

      Founding Partner

      Charles Hamilton Ferguson is a founding Partner at ReedHamilton. With a vast network of senior executives across Asia, status in the market as a cloud computing expert, and reputation as a trusted advisor amongst his peers, he brings his experience and technology leadership to the fore as the lead for ReedHamilton’s technology and communications industries coverage for the region. He has over 17 years of experience in the information technology industry working in Mainland China, Hong Kong, Singapore, and Indonesia with 22 years living in Asia.

      Prior to co-founding ReedHamilton, Charles was regional vice president for SAP on demand, cloud services, and outsourcing, Asia Pacific and Japan. In this capacity, he was responsible for helping design, deploy, and general manage SAP’s SaaS business and cloud computing evangelism for the region. Previous to this role he led SAP’s Southeast Asia and emerging markets and technologies marketing organization. In 2006, he joined salesforce.com as the North Asia regional director where he built and led the team in new market penetration efforts for Hong Kong SAR, Macau SAR, PR China, and Taiwan ROC. Previous to SAP and salesforce.com, he held senior sales, consulting, and services roles at Microsoft and Intel.

      Charles attended Bard College in the United States and is currently pursuing a masters degree in Innovation Management from the Helsinki School of Economics. He is fluent in Mandarin Chinese, and is an advanced speaker of Shanghai dialect, Malay, Indonesian.
